+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
|           ACCIÓN            |                          COMANDO                                   |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Acceder al contenedor       | docker exec -it nginx sh                                           |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver configuración principal | cat /etc/nginx/nginx.conf                                          |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver sitios disponibles      | ls /etc/nginx/sites-available                                      |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver sitios habilitados      | ls /etc/nginx/sites-enabled                                        |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Probar configuración        | nginx -t                                                           |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Recargar configuración      | nginx -s reload                                                    |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Reiniciar Nginx             | nginx -s reopen                                                    |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Detener Nginx               | nginx -s stop                                                      |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver procesos activos        | ps aux | grep nginx                                                |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver logs de acceso          | tail -f /var/log/nginx/access.log                                  |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Ver logs de errores         | tail -f /var/log/nginx/error.log                                   |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Editar archivo de sitio     | vi /etc/nginx/sites-available/default                              |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
| Crear enlace simbólico      | ln -s /etc/nginx/sites-available/default /etc/nginx/sites-enabled/ |
+-----------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------+
